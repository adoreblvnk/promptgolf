Registration: Closed

## About

The era of simple chatbots is over. Real AI applications need to scrape live data, automate workflows, manage infrastructure, and deploy at scale -- all working together in production. If you're ready to build real AI systems, this hackathon is for you.

AI Builders is partnering with SMU AI for Agent Forge Hackathon in Singapore -- a hands-on, full-day hackathon where you'll prototype production-grade AI applications using tools from sponsors.

## What You'll Do

- Build a working AI project in one day using sponsored tools and APIs.
- Attend a hands-on workshop to get up to speed before hacking begins.
- Form teams of up to 6 people or go solo.
- Demo your project live and compete for sponsor credit prizes.
- Connect with builders, engineers, and founders in the Singapore AI community.

## Your AI Stack

### Bright Data

[Bright Data](https://brightdata.com/?utm_source=luma) gives your assistant the ability to browse the web. Build custom web-scraping "skills" into your agents to fetch real-time data, monitor trends, or aggregate research automatically using reliable, large-scale infrastructure.

- Credits: [http://get.brightdata.com/aibuilders10](http://get.brightdata.com/aibuilders10?utm_source=luma)

### Daytona

[Daytona](https://www.daytona.io/?utm_source=aibuilders) is an open-source, secure infrastructure platform providing fast, isolated, and stateful composable computers, or sandboxes, for AI agents to safely run, test, and develop code. Each hackathon participant will get USD 100 worth of Daytona credits to build.

- Credits: [https://www.theaibuilders.dev/daytonacredits](https://www.theaibuilders.dev/daytonacredits?utm_source=luma)

### Kimi AI

[Kimi AI](https://platform.kimi.ai/docs/overview?utm_source=luma) provides the `kimi-k2.6` API, featuring SOTA coding, cost-efficient context caching, and native agent swarm execution to empower developers building complex, multi-step hackathon solutions.

- Credits: [https://www.theaibuilders.dev/kimicredits](https://www.theaibuilders.dev/kimicredits?utm_source=luma)

### Nosana

[Nosana](https://nosana.com/?utm_source=luma) supercharges compute with decentralized GPUs to run specialized AI models. Use it to run tasks, process heavy data workloads, or host custom local models for your assistant affordably.

- Credits: [https://www.theaibuilders.dev/nosanacredits](https://www.theaibuilders.dev/nosanacredits?utm_source=luma)

### SenseNova U1

[SenseNova U1](https://github.com/OpenSenseNova/SenseNova-U1?utm_source=luma) is an open-source, native multimodal AI model that unifies text understanding, reasoning, and high-fidelity image generation within a single, end-to-end architecture. Its SenseNova U1 Skills provide modular expert capabilities to automate complex Excel analysis, PowerPoint creation, and deep research.

- Credits: [https://www.theaibuilders.dev/sensenovacredits](https://www.theaibuilders.dev/sensenovacredits?utm_source=luma)

### Terminal 3 Agent Dev Kit

[Terminal 3's Agent Dev Kit](https://www.terminal3.io/products/ai?utm_source=luma) allows developers to equip AI agents with verifiable identity to act and transact on behalf of users without compromising sensitive data. Built on the Terminal 3 Network's hardware-secured TEEs, it provides autonomous AI workflows with the trust and security guarantees they need.

- Credits: [https://www.terminal3.io/products/agent-developer-kit](https://www.terminal3.io/products/agent-developer-kit?utm_source=luma)

### TokenRouter

[TokenRouter](https://www.tokenrouter.com/?utm_source=luma) is a unified routing platform offering seamless access to the top AI models worldwide. It delivers faster, better, and cheaper performance via smart caching.

- Credits: [https://tinyurl.com/tokenroutercredits](https://tinyurl.com/tokenroutercredits?utm_source=luma)

### VideoDB

[VideoDB](https://videodb.io/?utm_source=aibuilders) is data infrastructure for video, built for machines and agents. It turns camera feeds, live streams, files, and recordings into searchable context, real-time alerts, clips, and agent workflows.

- Credits: [https://www.theaibuilders.dev/videodbcredits](https://www.theaibuilders.dev/videodbcredits?utm_source=luma)

Your project must integrate the sponsored products. This is a chance to go hands-on with tools used by production AI teams.

## Prizes

Winning teams share sponsor credits as prizes. All participants receive credits from sponsors to build with during the hackathon.

## Judging Criteria

- **Completeness:** Whether the team finished at least a minimum viable product.
- **Innovation:** Is the product idea innovative?
- **Real-Life Problem Solving:** Is the project solving a real-life problem, or is there a real pain in the market?
- **Sponsored Product Usage:** Did the team use sponsored products?
